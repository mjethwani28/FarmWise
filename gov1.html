PMFBY

The Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) is a government-backed crop insurance scheme in India that aims to support sustainable production in the agriculture sector. It provides financial support to farmers who suffer crop loss or damage due to unforeseen events. The scheme has several objectives, which are outlined as follows: a) Providing financial support to farmers: PMFBY offers insurance coverage and compensation to farmers who experience losses or damages to their crops due to natural calamities such as drought, flood, cyclone, pests, diseases, etc. The scheme ensures that farmers receive financial assistance to recover from such losses and continue their agricultural activities.
