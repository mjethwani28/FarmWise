Precision Agriculture

Precision Agriculture: Cultivating the Future, One Pixel at a Time.

Precision agriculture is a farming technique that uses technology such as GPS, sensors, and drones to optimize farming practices. Farmers can gather data on soil conditions, crop health, and weather patterns to make informed decisions regarding irrigation, fertilization, and pest management.
